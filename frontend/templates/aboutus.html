Fitness friend is your ultimate fitness companion

Fitness Friend is a workout tracker unique to you! Are you an experienced lifter? Create your own plans consisting of up to 8 lifts with custom set and rep amounts. New to lifting? We can help you create your own workout plan based on your goals with the click of a button using our trained machine learning model! Either way, you can track your lifts in our workout tracker. The workout tracker allows you to input the average reps, weight, and reps in reserve for each lift in your workout! Other features include our calander that displays all days you have tracked your workouts, our tracking history page that allows you to view all past workouts in detail, and our weight recommendation system that helps to ensure you consistently progressively overload!
